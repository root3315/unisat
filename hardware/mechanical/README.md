# Mechanical Design

CubeSat frame STEP models for different form factors.

## Form Factors
- `frame_1u.step` — 100 × 100 × 113.5 mm (1.33 kg max)
- `frame_2u.step` — 100 × 100 × 227.0 mm (2.66 kg max)
- `frame_3u.step` — 100 × 100 × 340.5 mm (4.00 kg max)
- `frame_6u.step` — 100 × 226.3 × 340.5 mm (12.00 kg max)

## Material
- 7075-T6 Aluminium alloy
- Hard anodized (black) for thermal control
- Compliant with CubeSat Design Specification Rev. 14

## Notes
STEP files require CAD software (FreeCAD, SolidWorks, Fusion 360) to view.
Generate from parametric model using the configurator.
